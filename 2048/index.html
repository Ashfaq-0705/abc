How to Play

Goal: Combine tiles with the same number to reach 2048!

  • Use Arrow Keys or Swipe to move tiles
  • When two tiles with the same number touch, they merge into one
  • After each move, a new tile (2 or 4) appears
  • Keep merging to reach higher numbers!

Tips:

  • Try to keep your highest tile in a corner
  • Focus on one direction (usually down or right)
  • Plan your moves ahead to avoid getting stuck
